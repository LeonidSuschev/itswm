TEAM MEMBERS

ITSWM is led by Managing Partner Ekaterina Popova. Our in-house team comprises a diverse group of experts in financial planning, risk analysis, law, and project administration. Additionally, our network of family office partners includes leading banks, investment houses, law firms, and consulting groups worldwide.

Our Commitment to Reputation

Our impeccable reputation is our most valuable asset. We take pride in being a reliable business partner, a responsible employer, and a conscientious member of the professional community. Our goal is to build long-term relationships with every family that becomes our client.

Teamwork

Fostering Excellence

We create a work environment rooted in trust, openness, and integrity, ensuring conditions that encourage responsible and efficient performance from our team. The long-term success of each project is shared by every team member, serving as both a reward and a motivation for continued professional growth.

OUR PARTNERS

Responsible Partnership

Our commitment to high standards, integrity, and the fulfillment of all obligations to our partners has led to the expansion of a unique network of experts around the world. Our partner network continues to grow each year, currently comprising over 50 partners in 20 countries.
